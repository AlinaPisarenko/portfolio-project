About

I'm a front-end developer originally from Russia, currently based in New York. I studied design and architecture at the State Technical University of Saratov. During last couple of years in school and after graduating, I was working as an interior designer. But I always had a dream about moving to another country, so in 2016 I moved to New York and I have been bartending since then. I've been interested in tech since the first year of my college, but only recently I realized that this is what I want to do for life, so I have been teaching myself via Shecodes, Codeacademy and Udemy.

I think of myself as a goal-oriented person who is easy to work with, a team-player, who can also work independently. Studying and working in architecture helped me become an organized and patient worker, while my bartending experience gave me the ability to work well under pressure and be able to start any conversation easily. I am committed to learning and self-development so that I can consistently achieve better results. I look forward to every single challenge coding will bring me. Just like architecture, an art that used by people daily, coding will let me create something that people will use, work and interact with on a daily basis. And I feel really excited about it!
